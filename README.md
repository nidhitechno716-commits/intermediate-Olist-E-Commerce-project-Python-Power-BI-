## Olist-E-Commerce-project-Python-Power-BI
## Dataset Source
The dataset used in this project is the Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset
provided by Olist and made available on Kaggle for public analysis and learning.

## An e-commerce company wants to understand:

Order and revenue trends
Customer distribution
Payment behavior
Delivery performance & delays

- The analysis shows a steady flow of orders across months, indicating consistent customer demand in the e-commerce platform.
- A small number of states contribute a significant portion of total revenue, highlighting key regional markets for the business.
- Credit card payments dominate as the most preferred payment method, followed by boleto, indicating customer preference for flexible payment options.
- The average delivery time is within acceptable limits; however, a noticeable percentage of orders are delivered later than the estimated delivery date.
- Late deliveries are more frequent in certain regions, suggesting potential logistics or fulfillment challenges.
- Freight cost contributes a meaningful portion to overall revenue, emphasizing the importance of optimizing shipping strategies.

These insights can help business stakeholders improve delivery performance, optimize regional operations, and enhance customer experience.
